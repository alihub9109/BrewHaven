Our Story

From Small Beginnings

Founded in 2012 by two coffee enthusiasts, Brew Haven started as a tiny kiosk in downtown Portland. Our passion for ethically sourced beans and expertly crafted beverages quickly gained us a loyal following.

Today, we operate multiple locations but maintain our commitment to quality and community. Every cup we serve reflects our dedication to the craft of coffee making and our respect for the farmers who grow these amazing beans.

We roast our beans in small batches to ensure peak freshness and flavor. Our baristas undergo extensive training to perfect their craft, because we believe that great coffee is both an art and a science.

Sarah & Michael Johnson
